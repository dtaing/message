Goldfinch

Secret Green Mission

  • One Red player and one Blue player must die (You do not have to personally kill them).

Character Abilities

  • 潛伏: Hidden Character
  • 潛藏伏擊: During your action phase, you may flip up this card to return one other player's message to the top of the library.
  • 黃雀在後: When another player dies, draw a card. You may also flip open and reveal your identity card; if you do, place up to two black messages from your hand onto one player's board.

Cultural Notes

  • Goldfinch's name (黃雀) is a reference to an old Daoist idiom "螳螂捕蟬，黃雀在後", which roughly translates to "The mantis stalks the cicada, unaware of the goldfinch behind". This idiom can be interpreted to mean "to pursue a narrow gain whilst unaware of the danger behind".
