Secret Green Mission

  • Collect three or more red messages.

Character Abilities

  • 揭露: When a transmitted message reaches you, you may choose to discard one black card from your hand; if you do, flip open the transmitted message. If the revealed message is black, draw two cards.
  • 真相: Whenever a player uses a Test card, you are able to first take a look at that Test card.
