Chairman

Secret Green Mission

  • Collect four or more red messages, or four or more blue messages.

Character Abilities

  • 潛伏: Hidden Character
  • 移花接木: When a transmitted message reaches you, you may flip up this character card. If you do, switch the transmitted message with a card from your hand.
  • 金蟬脫殼: Whenever a player uses a Substitute card or an Intercept card, flip down this character card.

Cultural Notes

  • Chairman's first ability, 移花接木, is an idiom that literally means "to graft flowers onto a tree". Roughly translated, it means "to surreptiously substitute one thing for another".
  • Chairman's second ability, 金蟬脫殼, is an idiom that literally means "the cicada sheds its carapace". Roughly translated, it means "to vanish leaving an empty shell", or "a crafty escape plan".
