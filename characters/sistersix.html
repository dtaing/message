Sister Six (AKA Death Nurse)

Secret Green Mission:

  • Collect six or more messages of any color.

Character Abilities:

  • 拷問: Whenever you Test another player, you may take a card from that player's hand.
  • 搜取: When a player dies, you randomly pick one of the cards from the dead player's hand, and can choose either to add that card to your hand or to your message board.
