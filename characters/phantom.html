Phantom

Secret Green Mission

  • When you die, reveal your hand. If your hand contains three or more red cards, you win.

Character Abilities

  • 潛伏: Hidden Character
  • 就計: If a player uses a Test card or a Lock-On card on you, you may flip up this card and draw two cards from the library.
  • 城府: When you are being Tested, you can conceal your identity. Basically, you can choose to execute any of the options listed on the Test card, regardless of your real identity.
  • 遺志: When you die, you may give all of the cards in your hand face down to another player. That player then randomly selects three cards from those face down cards and adds them to his message board.

Cultural Notes

  • Phantom's third ability, 遺志, rougly translates to "deathbed wish".
