Old Gold

Secret Green Mission

  • Your hand contains exactly three red cards and three blue cards.

Character Abilities

  • 潛伏: Hidden Character
  • 棄卒保帥: When your action card is Canceled, you may flip up this character card. If you do, draw five cards, and then place two cards from your hand in any order onto the top of the library.
  • 釜底抽薪: When you receive a fake message, you may place another fake message from your hand onto your own board. If you do, flip down this character card, and place one message each from your hand onto the boards of any number of players.

Cultural Notes

  • Old Gold's first ability, 棄卒保帥, is a move in chess where one's pawn is sacrificed in order to save the queen.
  • Old Gold's second ability, 釜底抽薪, is the Nineteenth Strategem of The Thirty-Six Strategems of the Art of War, an essay written by Tan DaoJi that illustrated a series of strategems used in politics, wars, and civil interactions. 釜底抽薪 roughly translates to "taking drastic measures to deal with a situation".
