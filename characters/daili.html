Dai Li

Secret Green Mission

  • Collect three or more blue messages.

Character Abilities

  • 笑裡藏刀: When another player receives a real message you sent out, you may also place a fake message from your hand onto that player's board.
  • 運籌帷幄: Whenever you receive a real message, you may draw one card from the library.

Cultural Notes

  • Dai Li is a reference to the real historical figure of the same name who served as the head of Chiang Kai-shek's Military Intelligence Service from 1928 to 1946.
