Xiao Bai (Little White)

Secret Green Mission

  • Be the first player to die. If another player has already died first, then the entire red team or the entire blue team must die.

Character Abilities

  • 喬裝: This character is considered both a male and a female.
  • 收買: During your action phase, you may select five cards from your hand to give to a player. If you do, transfer one message from that player's board to your own.
